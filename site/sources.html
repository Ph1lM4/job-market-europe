Sources

26 primary sources. Every number on the signals page traces to one of these.

Tier 1 — Official Statistics & Large-Sample Research

  • Ravio Compensation Trends Report 2026
    1,500+ European tech companies, Q3 2025 data. Hiring rates, attrition, salary YoY, seniority distribution by function and country (UK, DE, FR, NL, ES, SE). AI talent metrics, tenure, gender pay gap by funding stage.
  • Eurostat — Enterprise AI Adoption (isoc_eb_ai)
    33 European countries, 2021-2025. Percentage of enterprises (10+ employees) using at least one AI technology. Annual survey with mandatory national statistical office reporting.
  • Eurostat — ICT Specialists (isoc_sks_itspt)
    36 European countries, 2015-2024. ICT specialists as percentage of total employment. Labour Force Survey.
  • Eurostat — Employment by ISCO 2-digit
    35 European countries, 2023-2024 YoY change by ISCO-08 2-digit occupation group. Labour Force Survey.
  • OECD Employment Protection Legislation (EPL)
    26 European countries, Version 4 (2019). Strictness of employment protection for regular contracts. Scale 0-6.
  • Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2025
    49,191 respondents globally, 16,276 European. Median salary (USD), remote work %, AI threat sentiment, IC vs PM split, developer type distribution. Self-selected sample, June 2025.
  • LinkedIn Labor Market Report, January 2026
    Economic Graph Research Institute. 1.3B members. Hiring rate vs pre-pandemic by country and company size, AI jobs created 2023-2025, AI talent migration, hiring by function. Global with European country breakdowns (DE, FR, UK, NL).
  • ISC2 Cybersecurity Workforce Study 2025
    16,029 respondents globally. 424K European cybersecurity gap estimate. Annual study with documented methodology.

Tier 2 — Industry Reports

  • Atomico State of European Tech 2025
    Annual ecosystem report, November 2025. 4.6M tech workforce, 27K+ founders, $44B VC investment, talent migration, visa data. Dealroom and LinkedIn data, supplemented by founder survey.
  • GitHub Copilot Working Paper (Baird, Carpanelli, Xu, Xu)
    LinkedIn EG Working Paper No. 5, September 2024. n=24,517 firms, 65 time periods. Difference-in-difference analysis of GitHub Copilot adoption on SWE hiring. Finds +6.6% entry-level, +4.9% senior hiring increase.
  • Figma Design Hiring Report, February 2026
    2,500 respondents. 73% of design hiring managers require AI fluency. Design-specific hiring trends, remote split, tool adoption.
  • Bridge Group 2025 SDR Metrics Report
    ~600 B2B SaaS companies. SDR/BDR quotas, promotion rates, ramp times. SDR-to-AE promotion dropped from 34% to 16%.
  • McKinsey State of Marketing Europe 2026
    500 European marketing leaders. Gen AI as #1 marketing investment priority. Budget growth signals despite hiring contraction.
  • ENISA NIS Investments 2025
    1,080 respondents across essential/important entity categories. NIS2 compliance spending, cybersecurity staffing ratios.
  • Recruited.tech European PM Hiring Trends
    European PM-specific job market analysis. Junior/senior split, AI PM roles, remote distribution.
  • IAPP AI Governance Survey 2025
    78% of organizations plan to hire 1-10 AI governance professionals. EU AI Act compliance hiring intent.
  • Bluebird EU SaaS Salary Benchmarks 2025
    European SaaS recruitment specialist. Salary bands by role and seniority for UK, Netherlands, Germany. Covers BDR, AE, BDR Manager, CSM, and Marketing roles (Content, Demand Gen, PMM, Growth, ABM). Proprietary recruitment data.
  • Hays IT-Gehaltsreport 2025
    1,300 IT specialists surveyed, Q2 2025. Germany-specific salary data by function (Cybersecurity €73,700, Data & Analytics €68,100, Development €62,300), experience level, sector, company size, and Bundesland. Tier-1 staffing firm with extensive placement data.
  • Robert Half 2026 Salary Guide
    Multi-country salary guide (UK, DE, NL, FR). Marketing & Creative, Technology, and Admin/Operations categories. Based on actual placement data and 350,000+ third-party job postings (Textkernel). 25th/50th/75th percentile salary bands.
  • Revenue Operations Alliance 2024 Salary Report
    Community-driven European RevOps salary data. European average $96,577 (57% of US levels). Only source with explicit European RevOps salary breakout found.
  • ECA International Salary Trends 2025/2026
    200 multinational companies. Pan-European salary adjustment forecasts by country. Used for country-level salary growth calibration across commercial and marketing functions.
  • Signium — AI in Talent Management, February 2026
    Global executive search firm survey of senior HR leaders. 75% positive about AI impact on HR, only 5% achieved enterprise-level integration. 34% say HR teams lack skills to leverage AI, 26% say leadership has limited understanding of AI implications for talent strategy. Recruiting/sourcing identified as highest-impact area. 78% cite data privacy as primary governance concern.

Tier 3 — Supporting Sources

  • EMEA Labour Market Outlook, March 2026
    LinkedIn Economic Graph, 4 pages. Regional hiring trends for DE, UK, Nordics, Mediterranean. London youth unemployment (24.6%), German construction sector (+128%).
  • Indeed Hiring Lab (DE, FR, UK/IE)
    Three country-specific blog posts with job posting indices and application data, February-March 2026. Screenshots extracted.
  • Bitkom Startup Report 2025
    152 German tech startups surveyed. Non-representative but indicative. 82% AI adoption, 75% plan hiring, 22% would found abroad next time.
  • Baird, Ko, Gahlawat — Skill Signals Working Paper
    LinkedIn EG Working Paper No. 1, January 2023. US LinkedIn data 2015-2021. +10 skills reduces median employment gap by ~1 month. Context for skills section.
